TBC Leveling & Attunement Guide
Authored by Thurlan for <Relax> on Dreamscythe | TBC Anniversary

Mission Objective

Streamline the Level 60-70 process for our guild. This guide consolidates optimal dungeon routes, reputation caps, and the Kara attunement chain into an easy to follow guide.

Pre-stacking quests is a great bonus but not assumed in this guide.

Compiled using info from the GuideMMO Strategy Video, Biosparks TV, and various notes I've collected. A special thanks to Yumm for his help on the edits.

Pre-Patch Preparation

Consumables & Engineering Tools
Prep
TL;DR
  • Farm these items now to speed up dungeon clearing later.
  • Magic Dust: 30s CC for non-mages.
  • Magic Candle: Ranged pull tool for Paladins/Warriors.
  • Hit Gear: Keep it! Rating decays per level, but hit cap is vital for high-level bosses.

Utility Items

  • Magic Dust: Drops from Dust Devils in Westfall. Puts target to sleep for 30s. Invaluable for groups without a Mage/Rogue/Hunter for CC.
  • Magic Candle: Drops from Kobold Geomancers in Elwynn Forest (or AH).
    Use Case: High-range pulling tool for Prot Paladins/Warriors to safely pull packs without face-pulling the whole room.

Engineering Tools

  • Arcane Bomb: AoE Silence/Interrupt. Extremely high value in TBC heroics & raids due to the high density of caster mobs (Blood Furnace, Shattered Halls).
  • Requirement: Engineering Schematic.
  • Luxury Item: Can be pricey so stockpile for raids or use them sparingly in heroics.

Why Hit Rating?

  • Level Delta: In optimized dungeon groups, you are often fighting mobs 2-3 levels higher than you.
  • The Penalty: Miss/Resist chance increases drastically against higher-level targets.
  • Priority: Hit Rating is your highest priority stat for consistency. A tank that misses cannot hold threat; a DPS that resists deals zero damage.
  • Action: Prioritize Hit Rating on all pre-patch gear and early quest rewards.

Hellfire Citadel

Ramparts & Blood Furnace
60 - 62
TL;DR
  • Start with Ramparts for the quest item.
  • Grind Blood Furnace until Honored (5999/6000).
  • Lower level dungeons get you just shy of Honored. Higher level dungeons (67+) get you past Honored.
  • Do not quest yet: Save all open-world quests for Level 70 gold (approx 6k gold total).

The Approach

  • Start in Ramparts: It is the introductory dungeon. Mobs are weaker, but XP density is lower. Your goal here is specifically to loot the Ominous Letter from the final boss.
  • Switch to Blood Furnace: BF has higher mob density and better XP per hour. Stay here until Level 62 or Honored (5999/6000).
  • Do NOT Quest: Saving Hellfire Peninsula quests for max level converts the XP reward into gold, netting you hundreds of extra gold for your flying mount.

Reputation Cap Mechanics

  • The Cap: Low level normal dungeons only grant reputation up to 5999/6000 Friendly. Once you hit this, you get 0 rep per kill. Higher level normal dungeons (67+) allow you to get past Honored.
  • Optimization: Stop running these dungeons exactly when you hit Honored. Further runs waste reputation potential that you could get later via Heroics or Quests.
  • Rep Goal: Reach Honored (5999/6000) so you are Revered and ready for heroics @ 70.

The Slave Pens

Zangarmarsh Hub
62 - 65
TL;DR
  • The highest XP/Hour bracket.
  • Set hearth to Cenarion Refuge.
  • Grind Slave Pens repeatedly.
  • Avoid The Underbog unless you need specific loot.

The Efficiency King

  • XP Density: Slave Pens is small, linear, and packed with mobs. A good group can clear it in ~15-18 minutes yielding ~4 bars of XP.
  • The Skip: After the first boss, you can jump up a ledge to skip a nasty pack of trash mobs. This increases runs per hour significantly.
  • Why not Underbog? Underbog is larger, has more spread-out mobs, and dangerous mechanics (fears, giants). It yields roughly 2.5 bars compared to SP's 4.0.

Cenarion Expedition

  • Reputation: Like Hellfire, run this until Honored. However, because the XP is so good, it is worth running even after you cap reputation just for the leveling speed.
  • Quests: Pick up "The Umbrafen Tribe" right outside the instance portal.

Ring of Blood

Nagrand Arena (Highly Recommended)
Lvl 65
TL;DR
  • A 6-quest chain in Northern Nagrand.
  • Requires a full 5-man group.
  • Rewards a massive amount of XP and a weapon that will last you until level 70.

Why Run This?

  • XP Spike: Completing the full chain grants approximately 150,000 XP. In a good group, this takes about 20-25 minutes. That is faster than any dungeon.
  • No Pugs: Since you are already dungeon leveling with a group, fly there together. Doing this with randoms usually leads to failed pulls or waiting around.

Old Hillsbrad & Sethekk

Attunement Setup
66 - 68
TL;DR
  • You MUST clear Old Hillsbrad to unlock Black Morass.
  • You SHOULD clear Sethekk to get the Shadow Lab key.
  • After clearing Old Hillsbrad once, swap to Sethekk Halls until 68.
  • DO THIS: Quest run of Crypts for the agility/caster chest.

Old Hillsbrad Foothills

  • The RP Event: This dungeon involves a long escort of Thrall. If he dies, you fail. Assign the Healer to watch Thrall's health specifically.
  • Attunement Link: Completing this is the only way to enter The Black Morass. Black Morass is required for the Karazhan Key. Therefore, Old Hillsbrad is mandatory.
  • Reputation: Grants Keepers of Time rep. Essential for Tanks (Leggings/Enchants).

Sethekk Halls

  • The Key: The main reason to come here is the Shadow Labyrinth Key located in the chest behind the final boss, King Ikiss. Without this, you cannot enter Shadow Lab (Lvl 68+).
  • Difficulty: This dungeon has some annoying mechanics (Mind Control totems, Charm). Focus fire totems immediately.

Endgame Protocol

Level 68 - 70 Routing
68 - 70
TL;DR
  • Do not blindly grind.
  • Choose your dungeon based on your specific Rep needs or Group Comp.
  • Shattered Halls for Speed, others for Attunement/Rep.

Honor Hold & Fat Pulls

  • The Meta: Shattered Halls has the highest mob count of any 5-man. If you have a Protection Paladin and a Mage/Warlock, you can pull massive groups and AOE them down. This yields 5+ bars of XP per clear. Avoid if you are lacking solid AOE.
  • The Gate: Requires the Shattered Halls Key (from the Smith Gorlunk quest chain in Shadowmoon Valley) or a Rogue with 350 Lockpicking to open the front gate.
  • Reputation: Grants Honor Hold / Thrallmar rep all the way to Exalted.

Lower City & Attunement

  • Why run it? It is the only high-level dungeon for Lower City rep. You also need to run it once to get the First Key Fragment for Karazhan.
  • XP: Moderate. The pulls are smaller and slower than Shattered Halls, but steady.

Cenarion Rep

  • Why run it? To finish off your Cenarion Expedition rep to Exalted for the Earthwarden staff or other rewards. Also holds the Second Key Fragment for Karazhan.
  • Difficulty: Easier than Shattered Halls for melee-heavy groups (Warriors/Rogues) due to fewer massive packs.

Avoid for XP

  • The Problem: Black Morass is time-gated. You cannot pull faster; you have to wait for portals to open. This results in terrible XP/Hour (approx 1 bar).
  • When to run: ONLY run this to complete the Karazhan Attunement (Opening the Dark Portal) or if you desperately need a specific drop from the bosses. Do not grind this for levels.

Solo Operations

Mob Grinding Locations
Solo
TL;DR
  • Plan B: Use if you have no group or to avoid launch day crowds.
  • Rule #1: Kill everything. Do NOT quest (save for Lvl 70 gold).
  • Rule #2: Stock food/water heavily. Layer hop if contested.

Levels 61-63: Arakkoa

  • Locations: Veil Reskk & Raastok Glade.
  • Why: High density of humanoids (Netherweave/Silver).
  • Backup: Tuurem (lower density, watch for high lvl adds).

Levels 62-64: Elves & Orcs

  • Locations: Firewing Point & Bonechewer Ruins.
  • Loot: Firewing Signets (Aldor Rep).
  • Note: Firewing mobs are casters (squishy but hurt). Bonechewer is great for Alliance (near Allerian Stronghold).

Levels 64-67: Kil'sorrow Fortress

  • Location: Southern border of Nagrand.
  • Density: Extremely high. Includes roaming patrols and buildings.
  • Loot: Mark of Kil'jaeden (Aldor Rep).
  • Strategy: Clear outside, move into buildings. Fast respawns mean you rarely need to move far.

Levels 67-69: Manaforge B'naar

  • Location: Next to Area 52.
  • Logistics: Huge advantage being next to a bank/vendor hub. Minimal downtime.
  • Loot: Sunfury Signets (Scryer Rep).

Levels 69-70: Manaforge Duro

  • Location: Near Cosmowrench.
  • Strategy: Stay here until 70. Very dense mob packs.
  • Flight Path: Cosmowrench connects to Area 52 for quick banking.
